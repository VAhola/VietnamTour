16 Day Tour: Comprehensive Vietnam

Day 1: Hanoi

Our tour starts with a pick up from the airport with one of our fully airconditioned minivans. We'll take you and your luggage to your hotel; the rest of the day you are free to explore the City to your liking.

Day 2: Hanoi- Ha long bay

On morning of day 2 we'll drive to Halong Bay in a mini-van.In Halong Bay we transfer to a boat which takes around this beautiful UNESCO heritage area known for the impressive rock formations rising from the water. The night will be also spend on the ship with comfortable beds and ensuite bathrooms in all cabins.

Day 3: Halong Bay - Hanoi

We will start the day 3 with a breakfast with a view to one of the most beautiful sceneries in word. In the afternoon there is a chance to visit a large cave and swim or relax in the sun. Upon return to the harbor we will transfer you back to Hanoi where you have the rest of the evening to look around the city again.

Day 4: Hanoi-Ho Chi Minh City

Day 4 starts with a transport from your hotel to the airport. From there you will take a two-hour plane ride to Ho Chi Minh City. Our crew will be waiting for you at the arrivals hall and will bring you to your hotel in the city center. The rest of the day you will be free to indulge the many attractions and activities this lovely city has to offer.

Day 5: Ho Chi Minh City

After you Vietnam style breakfast at the hotel we will visit the Cu Chi tunnels with a local guide. You will have a chance to see this historically important site from close by as well as to visit a Vietnamese rubber plantation. Afterwards we will have a half day street food tour with giving you a great sample of south Vietnamese cuisine and accompanying drinks.

Day 6: Ho Chi Minh City - Hoi An

During the day you will have the final chance to explore Ho Chi Minh City on your own. At the evening we will pick you up from your hotel and drive to the train station for the overnight train ride to Da Nang from where we will drive you to your hotel in Hoi An.

Day 7-8: Hoi An

After the busy city visit, the historic Hoi An offers a chance to relax and enjoy the more peaceful pace of life. While the two days you spend here do not have a predefined program we do recommend you to take a trip to the old town to enjoy the small, cosy restaurants and they fresh food and drink options. We do offer bikes for the during of the stay in Hoi An so you will be able to see the surrounding area as well.

Day 9: Hoi an - Hue -Da Nang -Ninh Binh

After breakfast we head off to the three hour drive to the former Royal Capital of Hue, where we will visit the Hue Citadel, Forbitten Purple City and the Emperial City. We will enjoy a dinner here before driving towards Da Nang railway station from where we will take the sleeper train to Ninh Binh.

Day 10: Ninh Binh - Hoa Lu -Ninh Binh

After checking into the hotel we will head of to Hoa Lu, the first and eldest capital city of the country. After enjoying the beautiful ancient architecture and history we will have a light dinner before driving you to the bamboo bungalow you will be sleeping in.

Day 11: Ninh Binh

First thing in the morning we head over to the Hang Mua viewpoint where we will climb up about 20 minutes to enjoy the breathtaking scenario from above. For those afraid of heights or opting out from the steep climb up we arrange an alternative excursion to the Bich Dong Pagoda. Afterwards we will gather together again for a cycling tour around the country side of Ninh Binh

Day 12: Ninh Binh - Sapa

In the morning we start our 6 hour drive from Ninh Binh to Sapa in one of our mini vans. We will make a stop for lunch in the province of Yên Bái where we enjoy the authentic Vietnamese atmosphere before continuing our journey towards Sapa and our Spa hotel there.

Day 13: Sapa

After breakfast we head off to our day of trekking. Together with a local guide we visit a small village and their recently opened primary school. There we have a change to interact with the tribal people and see how their every day lives look like. In the afternoon you will have a chance to go up to the highest mountain of Vietnam. In the evening we bring you back to the hotel for some well deserved rest.

Day 14: Sapa - Hanoi

You will have the entire morning to enjoy Sapa on your own before we head off to the 5 hour drive to Hanoi. After checking in to the hotel and some relaxation we will head over for a dinner at the Skyline Bar and Restaurant. Enjoying the tasty food and a drink while looking over the nightly skyline of Hanoi marks a beautiful ending for the day of travelling.

Day 15: Hanoi

In the morning you will have free time to visit any of the multitude of attractions available in the capital city on your own. When the air starts cooling down it is time for some Vietnamese food, this time prepared by you yourself during a cooking class! After we have cooked the dishes it is time to sit down and enjoy the fruits of your hard work before we drive you back to the hotel.

Day 16: Hanoi (departure)

Day 16 marks the end our journey together. Our guide will be available to drive you from the hotel to the airport at required time for your journey back to home or your next destination.
