Welcome to Veera's Vietnam Tours Website! We are a reliable, well-established tour agency specialised in one and two week tours in Vietnam.

About us:

Veera's Vietnam Travels was founded in 2004 by a group of travel enthusiasts with special fondness to Vietnam. Over the past years we have been able to build a large network of contacts within Vietnam which enables us to provide superior package and customised travel expriences to our clients.

We currently offer two different package options for tours in Vietnam, one for two weeks travel and one for a week. Next to these, we can also arrange a custom package for customers looking for different lenght or type of travel. We are happy to schedule a call or a meeting with you to discuss your individual needs.

All our transports are fully air-conditioned and suitable for both wheelchairs and people travelling with buggies. We are happy to answer any questions you may have about your travel to Vietnam so please get in touch with us for more details. We are currently available via telephone and email and are working on implementing our social media accounts in the near future.
