8 Day Tour: Northern Vietnam at a glance

Day 1: Hanoi

Our Northern Vietnam at a glance tour starts with a pick up from the airport with one of our fully airconditioned minivans. We will take you and your luggage to your hotel; the rest of the day you are free to explore the City to your liking.

Day 2: Hanoi - Ha Long Bay

In the morning of day 2 we will pick you up from the hotel and take you back to the airport from where you will catch a domestic flight to Van Don. From Van Don Airport you will be transported to the boat for the cruise of Ha Long Bay and Lan Ha Bay. You will stay in a private cabin with ensuit bathroon and toilet and an option to order breakfast to bed for the next morning.

Day 3: Halong Bay - Lan Ha Bay

Day three will be spend on water, we will sail from Ha Long Bay to Lan Ha Bay. You will also have an option to got for a swim or do a bit of Kayaking during one of the stops we take.

Day 4: Lan Ha Bay -Ha Long Bay -Hanoi - Sapa

During the morning we sail back to Ha Long Bay, from where we drive back to Hanoi in one of our Mini vans. In the evening we will take the overnight train to Sapa. For this part of the journey you will be staying either at a first class cabin which fits two people or VIP cabin which fits four; the type of cabin will depend on how many people you travel with.

Day 5: Sapa

After checking into the Spa hotel in Sapa, we will enjoy a good filling breakfast before starting our day of trekking. Together with a local guide we visit a local village and their recently opened primary school where we have the change to interact with the village people and see how their every day lives look like. We will also visit the two biggest tribes of the area and learn about their customs and history. In the afternoon you will have a chance to go up to the highest mountain of Vietnam, Fansipan or just relax for the rest of the day. In the evening we bring you back to the hotel for some well deserved rest.

Day 6: Sapa -Hanoi

\You will have the entire morning to enjoy Sapa on your own before we head off to the 5 hour drive to Hanoi. After checking in to the hotel and some relaxation we will head over for a dinner at the Skyline Bar and Restaurant. Enjoying the fine-dining in Vietnam while looking at the nightime skyline of Hanoi is an experience not to miss out on!

Day 7: Hanoi

In the morning you will have free time to visit any of the multitude of attractions available in the capital city on your own. When the air starts cooling down it is time for some Vietnamese food, this time prepared by you yourself during a cooking class! After we have cooked the dishes it is time to sit down and enjoy the fruits of your hard work before we drive you back to the hotel.

Day 8: Hanoi (departure)

Day 8 marks the end ouf the Northern Vietnam at a glance tour. Our guide will be available to drive you from the hotel to the airport at required time for your journey back to home or your next destination.
